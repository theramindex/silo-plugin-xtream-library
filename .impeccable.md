## Design Context

### Users
Silo users browsing large Xtream Codes lineups on televisions, desktops, and tablets. They need to find, organize, and play live channels quickly without learning provider-specific catalog structure.

### Brand Personality
Native, cinematic, and calm. The plugin should feel like a first-party Silo surface rather than a separate administration tool.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use Silo's theme and interaction language with the compact, media-first organization of UHF. Favor dense but readable layouts, progressive disclosure, and strong focus states. Support both Silo light and dark themes; Midnight Cinema is the primary visual reference. Avoid generic dashboards, nested cards, and long undifferentiated forms.

### Design Principles
- Make large IPTV catalogs feel small through search, grouping, and progressive disclosure.
- Preserve provider truth while presenting it in user-friendly structures.
- Keep primary playback and browsing actions obvious; move configuration detail behind compact controls.
- Use Silo-native spacing, typography, colors, and accessible touch targets.
- Prefer fast, optimistic interactions with clear saved/error feedback.
