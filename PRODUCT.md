# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Silo users and administrators who want a Dispatcharr-backed Live TV experience inside Silo. Users browse live channels, guide data, favorites, and playback controls; administrators configure the Dispatcharr or Xtream connection.

## Product Purpose

This plugin provides a Silo-hosted Live TV app backed by Dispatcharr. Success means users can quickly find and watch live channels without feeling like they have left Silo, while administrators can keep the connection and sync tasks understandable.

## Brand Personality

Focused, familiar, media-native. The interface should feel like a serious TV app embedded in Silo, not a separate management console.

## Anti-references

Avoid busy admin dashboards, marketing-style pages, decorative chrome, and duplicate navigation that makes the Live TV app feel detached from Silo.

## Design Principles

- Keep the user in the watching and browsing flow.
- Prefer familiar media-app patterns over invented controls.
- Keep navigation compact and task-focused.
- Surface state clearly without visual noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, clear labels, and reduced-motion-safe interactions for all playback and browsing surfaces.
